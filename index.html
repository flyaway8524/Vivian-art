About me

I am an illustration artist brimming with imagination and curiosity. I have an eye for all beautiful things in life and readily draws inspiration from delights around. Skilled in colors and lines, I expresses my love for life through simple and authentic artistic works.

I am passionate about people – what fancies them, what moves them, what inspires them and what motivates them. I enjoy talking to people and listen to their stories. Their excitement is as bright as sunshine. Their silence is as deep as ocean. Every time I pick up my oil pastel, a precious moment emerges from my memory, a flower blooms in a dream world.
